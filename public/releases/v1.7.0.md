# Version 1.7.0

## Process Analytics Center

Get a clear picture of your automation's business impact with the new Analytics Dashboard. See exactly how much time and money your workflows are saving, and track performance trends over time.

### ‚ú® New Features

- **Analytics Dashboard**: A dedicated page showing your automation ROI at a glance. Access it from the main navigation under "Analytics".

- **Business Impact Metrics**: View four key performance indicators showing total time saved, money saved, number of executions, and success rate - each with trend arrows comparing to the previous period.

- **Trends Over Time**: Interactive chart displaying your savings history. Toggle between time saved and money saved views, with separate scales for each metric so you can see the details clearly.

- **Smart Filtering**: Filter your analytics by time range (7 days to 1 year), specific workflows, projects, or tags to focus on what matters most.

- **Performance Insights**: See how fast your workflows run with percentile breakdowns (P50, P90, P99) to identify optimization opportunities.

- **Value Breakdown**: Understand which workflows deliver the most value with ranked tables showing top performers by time and cost savings.
